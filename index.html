About Bayside Bachata

Built for people who want to learn, dance, and meet the scene.

Whether you are stepping into your first class or looking for your next favorite social, Bayside Bachata is focused on creating a space that feels approachable, upbeat, and connected to the local Latin dance community.

The goal is simple: good music, strong instruction, and dance nights that make it easy to come back.

Why People Come

  • Beginner-friendly energy without losing social dance quality
  • Bachata and salsa in one community
  • Events centered around connection, rhythm, and fun
  • Easy ways to stay updated through social media
